Sinner's & Saint's

Bar & Grill

Here at Sinner's & Saint’s Bar & Grill, our staff strives to deliver the best service in the restaurant industry. We also take pride in providing a fun and friendly family atmosphere since 2010, allowing us to deliver the most reasonable prices for our mouth-watering cuisine. Located in the heart of downtown Portland, we bring you the best entertainment, and dining, at the best value. Bring your friends and family, there is something for everyone!

We fully support the local music scene. We regularly have acts like Sugarcane String Band, Ashbury Park, Mama’s Cookin’, Jared Mees and the Grown Children, Smoochknob, The Angry Oorts, and Big Metal Rooster. National and international acts also visit us on occasion. We recently hosted music from String Cheese Incident, Slightly Stoopid, and AfroCubism. Upcoming events include David Grisman, Ratdog, and DJ Logic.

Oregon is home to an amazing array for craft breweries. We our proud to support these local businesses, and have 25 local brews on tap, as well as many more in bottle and can. We have favorites like Deschute’s Brewing, Hood River Brewing, Rogue River Brewing, and many more. We truly do have something for everyone.
